This repository contains a comprehensive interactive dashboard built in **Microsoft Excel** designed to visualize and analyze **customer purchase and shipping data** for a retail business. The dashboard provides quick, actionable insights into sales trends, product performance, customer behavior, and operational logistics for the years 2023 and 2024.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features & Insights

This dashboard serves as a single source of truth for tracking customer purchase activity.

### üí∞ Financial & Inventory Summary

* **Total Customer Purchases:** Tracks the aggregate purchase value (e.g., $5,78,951).
* **Total Items Sold:** Monitors the volume of units sold (e.g., 10,745 items).
* **Category Performance:** Breakdown of purchase value across major customer segments: **Accessories, Clothing, Footwear, and Outerwear.**

### üìà Customer and Sales Analysis

* **Top Purchased Products:** Identifies the most popular items driving sales (e.g., Sweaters, Sunglasses, Shirts).
* **Purchase by Size:** Analyzes the distribution of purchases across different product sizes (S, M, L, XL), vital for inventory management.
* **Quantity by Colour:** Provides insight into customer preferences for product colors.

### üìä Time-Series Trends

* **Year-on-Year Comparison:** A trend chart comparing monthly customer purchase performance between 2023 and 2024 to spot growth or decline.
* **Quarterly/Seasonal Analysis:** Visualizes purchase volume grouped by seasons (Fall, Spring, Summer, Winter) to inform seasonal stocking decisions.
* **Monthly Purchase Value:** Shows the total purchase amount generated each month, highlighting peak and off-peak sales periods (e.g., December).

### üí≥ Operational Insights

* **Payment Percentage:** Tracks the usage frequency of various payment methods used by customers (Bank Transfer, Credit Card, PayPal, etc.).
* **Frequency Count:** Analysis of customer transaction frequency (Weekly, Monthly, Annually, etc.), offering insights into purchasing habits.
